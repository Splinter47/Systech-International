Simon is our Regional Director for the Middle East and is responsible for our offices in Dubai, KSA and Kuwait.

Simon has over 20 years experience in the construction industry and has worked in the Middle East for the last 5 years. In that time he has been involved with 2 multi-billion USD developments in the high rise and retail sectors. In addition Simon’s experience in the region covers petro-chemicals, oil & gas, and civils work. He has been working with projects in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia since June 2009.

Simon is proficient in all aspects of dispute resolution, contract and claims work, and has experience with mediation and arbitration.

Previous to moving to the Middle East Simon worked for a main contractor as a project manager and commercial manager.

Simon is a qualified Quantity Surveyor and a member of NZIQS.
