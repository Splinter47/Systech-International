Simon joined Systech International in 2008 and prior to this Simon has held directorships and senior commercial management positions for Contractors and Subcontractors in the UK.

Simon has over 20 years’ experience in commercial and contractual management and specialises in the preparation of claims and cost claims. Simon has vast experience in construction disputes on major international projects including civil engineering, building, rail and mechanical and electrical and plumbing.

Simon has been involved in arbitrations in the MEA and supporting teams of construction professionals and lawyers in dispute resolution strategies. Simon has also been involved in numerous adjudications in the United Kingdom.

Simon is a qualified Quantity Surveyor and Estimator, and is an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators.
