Dr Anthony is a civil engineer with legal qualifications and has over 20 years PQE experience. He provides expert reports and testimony on delay and quantum issues across Asia, particularly at international arbitrations, Singapore arbitrations, the Singapore Supreme Court and the Singapore Mediation Centre for Adjudication.

He operates in a range of sectors including construction, rail, airports, highways, oil and gas, marine, power and industrial process. Dr Anthony also provides project management, construction management and contracts management services. He is a regular speaker at conferences and a lecturer for university law and construction programmes.
